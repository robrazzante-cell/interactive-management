How It Works

A proven methodology for turning group input into structured action plans

1

Generate Ideas

Gather perspectives from participants using structured idea generation around a triggering question.

2

Structure Relationships

Use pairwise voting to determine how ideas relate to each other through Interpretive Structural Modeling.

3

Visualize Results

Generate hierarchical flowcharts showing priority levels and relationships between factors.
